Rule-Based Biology

Biology has accumulated vast amounts of data, yet remains unable to explain its own foundations. The limitation is not experimental — it is structural.

This work presents a rule-based framework for understanding biological systems. It replaces descriptive interpretation with logical structure, enabling clarity, consistency, and mechanistic reasoning.

Summary
Biology becomes understandable when governed by rules. This site explores what follows from that premise.
